Fetal Health Classification Using AI&ML

Our approach utilizes CTG scan data as the primary source for training our model, enabling us to develop a highly accurate and effective fetal health classification system that can provide valuable insights and diagnoses for healthcare professionals, all while providing an intuitive and user-friendly front-end interface for seamless interaction.

About

Our project is at the forefront of revolutionizing fetal health classification, leveraging machine learning and artificial intelligence to provide highly accurate and efficient diagnoses. We train our model using CTG scan data and employ a rigorous evaluation process to ensure maximum reliability. With an intuitive front-end interface, our system delivers crucial insights for healthcare professionals and empowers patients with valuable information.
